How Does TelePhysician App Work

Download the App

Get the app from the App Store or Google Play and install it on your device in seconds.

Create an Account

Sign up using your email or phone number and set up your personal health profile securely.

Choose Your Role

Select whether you’re a patient or a doctor to unlock features tailored to your needs.

Verify Your Details

Complete basic verification to ensure your consultations are safe and trusted.

Browse Specialists

Search through verified doctors by specialty, location, or availability to find the right match for you.

Book a Consultation

Schedule an appointment instantly — choose video, audio, or in-app chat at your convenience.

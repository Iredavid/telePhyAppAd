What is TelePhysician?

An app that connects you with licensed doctors through secure video, voice, or chat consultations.

 How do I talk to a doctor?

Simply download the app, create an account, choose a specialty, and book a session. You can consult via video, voice call, or chat depending on your preference.

 Do you accept insurance or health plans?

Yes. We partner with major insurance providers so you can use your plan directly within the app.
